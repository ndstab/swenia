swenia — design directions

New · refined lane F–K · typography-led restraint

F · Swiss Grid

open full ↗

Strict Archivo-grotesk system on near-monochrome paper; one hairline grid origin and a single micro-accent vermilion dot/rule — engineered restraint, not decoration.

G · Mono-Technical

open full ↗

IBM Plex Mono metadata, labels and tier on warm-white paper with one muted brick-red accent — a refined technical journal, not a dark hacker terminal.

H · Muji Quiet

open full ↗

Deck as paper, not UI: a single Newsreader title in enormous oatmeal space, tier reduced to one near-invisible terracotta dot, hairlines the only chrome.

I · Muted Editorial

open full ↗

Warm-paper, typography-led: a characterful Fraunces title and italic “why it matters” lede, with a single dusty-clay accent as quiet punctuation — never a block.

J · The Brief

open full ↗

Warm-paper editorial surface in Newsreader for title, why-it-matters and body, with Inter for quiet metadata; one calm deep-teal accent used sparingly.

K · Warm Modern

open full ↗

Warm-neutral bone/sand/soft-ink palette; Newsreader serif masthead against Inter body with one quiet terracotta accent and soft layered shadows.
